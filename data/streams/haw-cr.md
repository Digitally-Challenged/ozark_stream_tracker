## Description
Haw Creek runs II+ to III, solid intermediate water for paddlers who've moved past beginner runs but aren't chasing waterfalls yet. The kind of creek that teaches you how to move through a rapid instead of just surviving it.

What else it has to offer, you'll need to find someone who knows it better than paper does.

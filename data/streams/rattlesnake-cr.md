## Description
Some folks call it Rattlesnake Creek, others Rattlesnake Hollow, but either way it runs class IV water that'll test what you've learned on easier creeks. The name alone ought to tell you to pay attention. This is expert water, the kind where you scout from shore and commit from your boat, and there's no turning back once you've made the choice.

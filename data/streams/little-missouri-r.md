## Description
The Little Missouri runs Class II to III, which puts it right in that learning zone where mistakes teach you something without breaking anything important. River's wide enough to give you options, pushy enough to make you use them.

This is where novice paddlers become intermediate ones - not because the water's especially hard, but because it asks the right questions at the right volume.

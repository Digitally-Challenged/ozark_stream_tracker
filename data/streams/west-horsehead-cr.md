## Description

The West Fork of Horsehead Creek runs hard and fast when it runs at all - class IV pushing into the plus range. This is the meaner sibling of the regular Horsehead run, steeper gradient and less forgiveness built in.

Creek water with an attitude. You'll want solid boat control and a good crew before you point downstream.
